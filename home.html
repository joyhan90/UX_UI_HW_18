Hi, I am a product designer obsessed with customer behavior and understanding motivations. I worked in advertising and social media design in Brooklyn before moving to Los Angeles in 2015.

Empathy is my super-power and my dream is to create products which my boomer parents can use without calling me for help.

Outside of design, I am passionate about women’s health, a designated event-planner for friends and family, and Korean restaurant critic. I am currently trying to pick another language to learn among Spanish, French or coding. Let me know if you have suggestions!
